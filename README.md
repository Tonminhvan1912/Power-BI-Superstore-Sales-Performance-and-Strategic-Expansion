# [Power BI] Superstore Sales Performance and Strategic Expansion

## 1. **Introduction**

Developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard to provide senior management with critical business insights for market expansion strategies and strategic product selection.

### **1.1. Dataset**

The dataset stores global sales information for a company called Superstore. It consists of 03 data tables:

- **Orders**: a table that records transaction information.
- **People**: a table that contains information about salespeople in each region.
- **Returns**: a table that records returned transactions.

## 4. **Recommendations**

**Market**

- **Focus:** Given the high revenue potential in the **APAC, EU, US**, the company should invest in marketing and distribution efforts in countries like **Australia, France, Germany, and the US**.
- **Further Research on China:** Focusing on **China** presents a significant opportunity for our company due to its **high profit margins**. As one of the largest consumer markets in the world, China offers immense potential for growth across various sectors, particularly in technology and office supplies.

**Product**

- Furniture: Bookcases, Chairs

Target Segment: Consumer, Corporate
